Meet The Team

We’re here to help you stand out. Our team of innovative designers and creative storytellers are passionate about building and growing your web presence to reach its full potential. We are based in Harrisonburg, Virginia, and have been serving clients for 10 years throughout the Shenandoah Valley, DC Metro area, around Virginia, and around the country.

Joanna Bergdoll

Director Of Operations

Joanna is a people and relationship-centered person who brings diverse and extensive experience to her role as director. She is an extremely patient, empathetic leader who strives to achieve unrealized potential in the people and projects she works with. Joanna is an aspiring novelist and enjoys writing and painting in her free time.

  • If I could have a super power, it would be the power of healing.
