In The Beginning

Immerge was founded in 2005 as a software company in Harrisonburg, VA by a small group of recent college graduates. The company was creating immersive technology by casting interactive video across multiple screens. Not long after the founding of the company, the team realized that there was a real need and potential for web development within the community. Utilizing the skills of the team they began to develop client websites and grow their business.

Growth

In 2010, McClung Companies, a 70-year-old printing business located in Waynesboro, VA, acquired Immerge. McClung is constantly searching for better ways to serve their clients and realizes the advantage of a strong online presence. By taking on Immerge as the company’s web division, McClung is able to provide a range of marketing services to existing and future clients.

Partnership

Our clients need more than a one-time website – they need a partner to ensure an online presence that grows with their organization. Over the past eight years, Immerge has added numerous service offerings including search engine optimization (SEO), pay-per-click (PPC) marketing, and content creation. A website is a powerful tool, but if people can’t find it, it might as well not be there. A website should be a living and breathing extension of your business, and we want to make sure that your website is being utilized to its fullest potential.
